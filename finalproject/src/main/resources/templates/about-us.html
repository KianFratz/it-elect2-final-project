Our Story

In the cozy oven of 2015, a delightful little dream began to rise. With just a laptop, a generous scoop of passion, and a pinch of daring, our founder whipped up the very first batch of what would soon become PastryHave — baked fresh in a tiny apartment kitchen.

From those humble beginnings, we've grown layer by layer, like a perfect mille-feuille — crisp, colorful, and full of charm. At CuteCompany, we believe joy is the secret ingredient to everything. That’s why our office feels more like a candy shop than a cubicle farm — complete with bright colors, plushy bean bags, and a bottomless cookie jar.

Our mission? To make the world a cuter place, one sweet project at a time. Whether we’re crafting, coding, or collaborating, we always serve up high-quality work with a generous helping of smiles. So come on in — we’re already preheating the oven just for you.
